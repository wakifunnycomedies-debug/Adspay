Frequently Asked Questions

How many accounts can I have?

Only one account per person is allowed. Multiple accounts will lead to a permanent ban.

Why is my withdrawal still pending?

Withdrawals are manually verified to ensure ad integrity. This process usually takes 24-48 hours.

I didn't receive points for an ad. Why?

Make sure you stay on the page until the timer hits 0. Using a VPN will also block your rewards.
